Grandma's Mac and Cheese

Description and Recipe

Every Christmas holiday I bake this amazing macaroni and cheese side dish. My family loves it. No one in the family can bake it as good as I can!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Macaroni
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the over to 400 degrees
  2. Cook the macaroni according to the box instructions
  3. Pull the macaroni in a pan
  4. Add the cheddar, milk, and mustard to the macaroni
  5. Bake the macaroni and cheese for 1 hour covered with foil

Crispy Potatoes

Description and Recipe

Crispy potatoes are a staple in my family's household

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Olive Olive
  • Parsley
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Cut the potates into fourths
  3. Cover the potatoes with olive oil and Parsley
  4. Bake the potatoes in the over for 45 minutes or until crispy
